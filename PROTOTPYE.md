# Project Protoype

## Minimum Viable Product:
 ### Features:
 - Allow teachers to create assignments;
 - Assignments can be created, shared with students via qr code, and submitted by students;
 - Submitted assignments are stored and checked using AI Model on instructors side;
 - AI model in assingment to provide students with suppport and help;
 - Information on performance across assignments and questions avilable for Instructors;
 
 ### Feature Details:
 - Assignments are organized in questions and readings;
	- Each question can have visual elements, descriptions, input fields, and points;
	- Readings can have same visuals and text areas, as well as comment features;
	- LLM Chat feature to ask questions
	- Settings are available controlling properties such as:
		- Attempts allowed; level of AI assistance; 
		- Display/ hide correctness of submitted answers; 
		- How many attempts are allowed, score visibility; 
		- Degree to which LLM model assists students with answering; 
	- Database to store students and assignment submissions
	
 ### After prototype:
	- Account system for Instructors and students:
		- Instructors sign in with accounts while students can sign in or enter their students details on the assignment
	- Comment section to allow students to ask questions about the assingment; can be toggled by instructor to make visible to instructor/class only.
